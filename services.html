Services

Lawn Care

Lawn maintenance and servicing:

  • Mowing: Regular mowing promotes healthy growth, prevents weeds, and keeps your lawn looking neat. You can specify mowing frequency options based on the season and grass type.
  • Edging: Clean edges define your lawn and garden beds, creating a polished look.
  • Trimming: Trimming keeps hedges, shrubs, and small trees in shape and under control.
  • Weeding: Effective weed control prevents unwanted plants from stealing nutrients and water from your lawn. You can offer both pre-emergent and post-emergent weed control solutions
  • Fertilization: Proper fertilization nourishes your lawn with essential nutrients, promoting healthy growth and vibrant color. You can highlight customized fertilization plans based on soil tests
  • Aeration: Aeration improves soil compaction, allowing air, water, and nutrients to reach the grass roots.

Tree and Shrub Care

  • Pruning: Regular pruning promotes healthy growth, shape, and size control. You can offer different types of pruning like thinning, heading back, and rejuvenation pruning.
  • Tree Removal: Safely removing dead, diseased, or hazardous trees.
  • Tree Trimming: Removing dead or damaged branches, reducing size, or improving clearance.
  • Fertilization: Providing essential nutrients for optimal tree health and growth.
  • Disease and Pest Control: Identifying and treating common tree problems to prevent damages
  • Cabling and Bracing: Supporting weak branches to prevent breakage.

Shrub Care

  • Pruning: Shaping, rejuvenating, and maintaining shrub size and health.
  • Fertilization: Providing necessary nutrients for lush growth and blooming.
  • Disease and Pest Control: Identifying and treating common shrub problems.
  • Planting: Installing new shrubs for landscape enhancement.

Planting

Garden Maintenance

  • Weeding: Regular removal of unwanted plants to prevent competition for nutrients and water.
  • Mulching: Applying a layer of organic material to retain moisture, suppress weeds, and enrich the soil.
  • Trimming: Maintaining the shape and size of plants, including shrubs, hedges, and perennials.
  • Deadheading: Removing spent flowers to encourage continuous blooming.
  • Fertilizing: Providing essential nutrients to promote healthy plant growth.
